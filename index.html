About Us

Manavya Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting underprivileged communities through education, healthcare awareness, and social welfare initiatives.Our goal is to create a positive and lasting impact in society by empowering people and improving theirquality of life.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote community development by encouraging environmental sustainability,improving sanitation,ensuring access to clean water,supporting women empowerment,protecting child welfare, and promoting the inclusion of persons with disabilities.

What We Do

We focus on enhancing the holistic well-being of vulnerable and marginalized communities through programs related to education, healthcare,livelihood development,nutrition,sanitation, mental health,and social welfare.

Our Work & Initiatives

Manavya Foundation designs and implements impactful social initiatives that aim to improve living standards,restore dignity,and promote sustainable development among economically weaker sections of society. Our efforts in education include supporting schools,learning centers, vocational training institutes,digital literacy programs,and scholarship opportunities for underprivileged students.

Healthcare Support

In healthcare,we organize medical camps,mental health awareness programs,and community health initiatives while helping people access affordable healthcare services.

Livelihood & Empowerment

We also support livelihood generation and economic empowerment through skill development programs and entrepreneurship support,ensuring long-term community growth.

Our Commitment

All activities undertaken by Manavya Foundation are charitable, lawful,and carried out without profit motive,in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.
